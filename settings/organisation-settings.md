# Organisation Settings

Configure your agency's Entry workspace.

---

## Accessing Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar navigation. Organisation settings are available to users with **Admin** role.

---

## General Settings

| Setting              | Description                                          |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Organisation name** | Your agency's display name                           |
| **Default timezone**  | Used for scheduling and display (default: AEST)      |

---

## Integrations

Manage your connected services from **Settings → Integrations**:

| Integration      | Purpose                        | Status        |
| ---------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------- |
| Outlook Email    | Email sync and sending          | Available     |
| HubSpot          | CRM sync                       | Available     |
| Rex CRM          | Property and contact sync       | Coming soon   |
| Website Chatbot  | AI chat widget                  | Available     |

Each integration shows its connection status, last sync time, and a connect/disconnect button.

---

## Team Management

Add and manage team members from **Settings → Team**:

1. Click **Invite Member**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select their role (Admin or Member)
4. They'll receive an email invitation to join your organisation

{% hint style="info" %}
Team members sign in with their own Google or Microsoft account. Each member has their own session and can connect their own Outlook mailbox.
{% endhint %}
