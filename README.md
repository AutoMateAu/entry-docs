# Welcome

## Entry Real Estate — Unified Communications Platform

{% hint style="info" %}
**Platform:** [app.askentry.com](https://app.askentry.com)\
**Built for:** Australian real estate agencies\
**Version:** Production
{% endhint %}

Entry is a unified communications platform purpose-built for Australian real estate agencies. It consolidates email, CRM, chatbot, and AI-assisted drafting into a single workspace — so your team spends less time switching tools and more time closing deals.

### What Entry Does

* **Unified Inbox** — All email threads from Outlook in one place, with AI-powered classification and priority scoring
* **AI Email Drafts** — Generate context-aware reply drafts using your agency's knowledge base
* **Knowledge Brain** — Upload your documents (listings, policies, FAQs) and Entry uses them to write accurate responses
* **HubSpot Sync** — Bidirectional contact and deal sync with your existing CRM
* **Website Chatbot** — AI-powered chat widget for your agency website, backed by your knowledge base
* **Client Management** — Track clients, leads, and VIP contacts across your organisation
