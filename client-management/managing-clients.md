# Managing Clients

Entry maintains a client database for your organisation, populated from email contacts, CRM sync, and manual entry.

---

## Client Sources

Clients enter Entry from multiple channels:

| Source       | How it works                                                |
| ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Email**     | Contacts extracted from incoming emails automatically       |
| **HubSpot**   | Bidirectional sync from your CRM                            |
| **Manual**    | Created directly in Entry by your team                      |
| **Chatbot**   | Captured from website chat conversations                    |

---

## Viewing Clients

Navigate to **Clients** in the sidebar to see your full client list. Each client record shows:

* **Name and contact details** — Email, phone, company
* **Tags** — Categorisation labels (e.g., "Property Buyer", "Vendor", "Tenant")
* **Source** — Where the client came from
* **Communication history** — Linked email threads and interactions

---

## Adding a Client

1. Go to **Clients**
2. Click **Add Client**
3. Fill in the required fields (name, email)
4. Add relevant tags to categorise the client
5. Click **Save**

---

## Editing and Tagging

Click any client record to edit their details or manage tags. Tags are used for:

* **Categorisation** — Buyer, Seller, Tenant, Landlord, etc.
* **Filtering** — Quickly find specific groups of clients
* **CRM sync** — Tags sync with HubSpot contact properties

---

## Search and Filter

Use the search bar and filter controls to find clients by:

* Name or email
* Tags
* Source
* Date added

{% hint style="info" %}
Client data is **organisation-scoped**. Each agency only sees their own clients. There is no cross-organisation data access.
{% endhint %}
