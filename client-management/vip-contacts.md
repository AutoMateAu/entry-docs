# VIP Contacts

Mark important contacts as VIPs to ensure their emails receive priority attention in the unified inbox.

---

## What VIP Does

When a contact is marked as VIP:

* Their emails receive a **higher priority score** in the inbox
* A **VIP badge** appears on their messages for quick identification
* They surface to the top of your inbox when new messages arrive

---

## Managing VIPs

### Adding a VIP

1. Go to **Clients** or open a client record
2. Click the **VIP toggle** or star icon
3. The contact is now marked as VIP

### Removing VIP Status

1. Open the VIP contact's record
2. Toggle VIP off
3. Future emails from this contact will receive standard priority scoring

---

## VIP in the Inbox

VIP emails are visually distinguished in the unified inbox:

| Indicator        | Description                              |
| ---------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **VIP badge**     | Coloured label next to the sender name   |
| **Priority boost** | Automatically scored higher in priority  |

{% hint style="success" %}
Use VIP for your most important clients — key vendors, high-value buyers, landlords with large portfolios. It ensures their communications are never buried in a busy inbox.
{% endhint %}
