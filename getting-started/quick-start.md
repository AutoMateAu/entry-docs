# Quick Start

Get your team up and running with Entry in three steps.

---

## Step 1: Sign In

Navigate to [app.askentry.com](https://app.askentry.com) and sign in with your Google or Microsoft account. Your agency admin will have already created your organisation and sent you an invite.

{% hint style="info" %}
Entry supports **Google OAuth** and **Microsoft OAuth** for single sign-on. You can also use email and password if your admin has enabled it.
{% endhint %}

---

## Step 2: Connect Your Email

Once signed in, connect your Outlook account to start syncing emails into the unified inbox.

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Outlook Email**
2. Click **Connect** and authorize Entry to access your mailbox
3. Entry will begin syncing your recent emails automatically

{% hint style="warning" %}
Only **Microsoft Outlook** (Exchange Online / Microsoft 365) is supported for email integration. Gmail support is planned for a future release.
{% endhint %}

---

## Step 3: Upload Knowledge

Give Entry the context it needs to draft accurate replies by uploading your agency's key documents.

1. Go to **Knowledge Brain → Documents**
2. Click **Upload Document** and select your files (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, CSV, TXT, or Markdown)
3. Entry will extract, chunk, and index the content automatically

Once uploaded, the AI will use these documents when generating email draft suggestions.
